I collaborated with my team for Wojciechowska Real Estate, a Poland-based company, to conceive and execute a new website. Our project involved the utilization of WordPress as our development framework, employing the Twenty Twenty-Three Theme in conjunction with custom HTML coding to craft an aesthetically pleasing and intuitive website. We integrated bespoke style elements, encompassing typography, color schemes, button design, and spacing, to harmonize with the company's branding. We also designed template parts to facilitate effortless page creation and reusability.
